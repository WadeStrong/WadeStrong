Competition Rules & Tactics
🎯 Order of competition
Athletes compete in reverse placing order — last place goes first, leader goes last. If you're leading after an event, you compete last in the next one and can watch everyone lift first. Use it.
📊 Attempt selection
  • Declare your next attempt immediately after each lift
  • Openers must be 95%+ certainty — a missed opener costs points AND confidence
  • Wade may adjust your thirds based on what others lift
🏅 Points & placing
Consistency beats heroics. A 2nd, 2nd, 2nd beats a 1st, 1st, bomb-out. Wade programs all events — not just your strongest.
💬 Wade's tactical notes
You're a contender in this field. If you podium event 1 you go last in event 2 — use it. Watch the top 3 and know your number before you touch the bar. Your strongest events are late in the day. Stay patient early.
Wade's Notes
